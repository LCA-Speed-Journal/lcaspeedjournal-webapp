# Cycle Drill
[VIDEO](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q14gaAK6o8Q&list=PLJYDxHAFIGuOJrfQDKCgg_FD5irEy309P&index=1&pp=iAQB)
- Hurdles placed 15 foot-lengths apart
- Cueing “heel to hip” for both leg
	- not a full trail-leg, just working on a high-knee cycling over top
- A Drill for Rhythm — not necessarily the movements over top of the hurdle
# One-Step Drill
[VIDEO](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s5Qcj3PQIS0&list=PLJYDxHAFIGuOJrfQDKCgg_FD5irEy309P&index=2&pp=iAQB)
- Hurdles placed 5-6 (can be 7) foot-lengths apart — depends on athlete and proficiency
- Lead leg not kicking forward, trail leg not going wide — working on heel to hip
- Another drill for rhythm and building the feel of carrying momentum between hurdles
# Cycle-Ladder Drill
[VIDEO](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ewKFeWQ30sE&list=PLJYDxHAFIGuOJrfQDKCgg_FD5irEy309P&index=3&pp=iAQB)
- Cycle Drill, but placing hurdles 17 foot-lengths apart — start at 11, then move 2 feet p/workout
- Working on a 3-step cadence in between the hurdles
- Driving trail leg down forcefully into the ground
# Jammed Hurdling
VIDEOS: [Part 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rLt1-ycYDRs&list=PLJYDxHAFIGuOJrfQDKCgg_FD5irEy309P&index=4&pp=iAQB) || [Part 2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4fOYdkcRj58&list=PLJYDxHAFIGuOJrfQDKCgg_FD5irEy309P&index=5&pp=iAQB) || [Part 3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q270IstsyKE&list=PLJYDxHAFIGuOJrfQDKCgg_FD5irEy309P&index=6&pp=iAQB)
- Begin with traditional spacing, but place next hurdles 4 foot-lengths closer
	- Work further as the athlete becomes more proficient — 3 foot lengths, 2, etc.
- Cues:
	- “Move the feet fastest before the first hurdle”
	- “Keep posture pressing forward” — not leaning back, loses momentum
- Key is to keep running off the hurdle, don’t “prepare to hurdle” — keep sprinting
# Seven-Stepping
VIDEOS: [Part 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cPaYItTSl4M&list=PLJYDxHAFIGuOJrfQDKCgg_FD5irEy309P&index=7&pp=iAQB) || [Part 2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J5gVjn_pV2k&list=PLJYDxHAFIGuOJrfQDKCgg_FD5irEy309P&index=8&pp=iAQB) || [Part 3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tuFv73KvD4Y&list=PLJYDxHAFIGuOJrfQDKCgg_FD5irEy309P&index=9&pp=iAQB)
- Key is to reaccelerate off the hurdle into that 7-step zone
- Set Hurdles up as normal (2-3 foot-length discounted spacing), then pull out Hurdle 4
	- Can progress by adding another hurdle back in the 3-step pattern
