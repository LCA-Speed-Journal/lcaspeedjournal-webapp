# Hurdle Drills Explained
- Walkovers — 4 Hurdles (~6 Steps between)
	- Lead: Vertical Projection, leading with bent-knee, strike down
	- Trail: Knee Higher than Foot, Toe Up and Out, Heel to Butt
- Skip-Overs — 4 Hurdles (~6 Steps between)
	- Working on rhythm between the hurdles
	- Trail: need to step past the hurdle before trail-leg comes thru
- Wall Drills — 3 Sets of 8-10 (or 2 Sets p/leg)
	- Height: lowest for MS and Girls, IM Height for Boys
	- Trail Leg: Slow and Purposeful
