# Distance Offseason — Block-2 — Ben Wurst

Block-2 settles the athlete into a routine: weights Tue/Thu, two main sessions per week plus long run. Focus on 3k rhythm and capacity, fartlek and progressions. **Test in Week 4:** 5×1k @ 3k.

**Ref. Paces (Block-2):** 3k 5:05–5:20/mi; 5k 5:30–5:40/mi; 5k–10k 5:40–5:50/mi; Tempo 5:55–6:05/mi; 10k 5:45–6:00/mi; HM 6:10–6:20/mi; M 6:30–6:40/mi; Easy > 8:45/mi, 7:30–8:00/mi; AE 6:50–7:20/mi; Long Run 7:30–8:45/mi; Steady 7:00–7:30/mi.

---

# Week 1 (2A)

Theme: Routine establishment; fartlek, 3k rhythm reps, aerobic short-intervals, capacity intervals. Weights Tue/Thu with optional AM-jog or easy run.

## Monday — Medium B: Fartlek-Style Intervals

**Theme:** Tempo and easy alternation; 20min total.

WU: 15–18min easy + 4 strides (2× 3k, 2× Mile).

**20min: (2min On Tempo, 1min Off Easy).**

CD: STR/Mobility C. 8 Reps or 15s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility A, then) Glute Kickback → Scorpions → Superman Raises.

**Ref. Paces:** Tempo 5:55–6:05/mi. Easy 7:30–8:00/mi.

## Tuesday — Weights (+ Optional AM-Jog or Easy Run)

**Theme:** Lower-body strength; optional aerobic maintenance.

AM-Jog or Easy Run: 20–35min (or 3–4mi) @ Easy  
————  
Weights (Lower Focus):  
4×5 Squat | 3×8 RDL | 3×10 Step-Ups  
3×12 Russian Twist | 3×15 Standing Calf

**Ref. Paces:** Easy > 8:45/mi.

## Wednesday — Workout B: 3k Rhythm Reps

**Theme:** 3k rhythm; 2 rounds of 6×200m.

WU: 18–20min easy + 4 strides (2× 3k, 2× Mile).

**2 Rounds of: 6×200m @ 3k.** Jog-Rest: 100m p/rep, 400m p/round.

CD: STR/Mobility D. 10 Reps or 20s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility C, then) Skipping → Lunge (FWD/Lateral) → Split SQ → V-Up.

**Ref. Paces:** 3k 5:10–5:20/mi.

## Thursday — Weights (+ Optional AM-Jog or Easy Run)

**Theme:** Total-body strength and power.

AM-Jog or Easy Run: 20–35min (or 3–4mi) @ Easy  
————  
Weights (Total-Body Focus):  
3×5 Hang Clean-Pull  
3×8 (Shoulder Press, Lat-Pull) | 3×10 (DB Bench, DB Row)  
2 × (Triple Broad-Jump | 3× SL Hop | 10s Line-Hop)

**Ref. Paces:** Easy > 8:45/mi.

## Friday — Medium B: Aerobic Short-Intervals

**Theme:** 5k-paced short reps; 4 rounds of 6×150m.

WU: 15–18min easy + 4 strides (2× 3k, 2× Mile).

**4 Rounds of: 6×150m @ 5k.** Jog-Rest: 50m p/rep, 400m p/round.

CD: STR/Mobility C. 8 Reps or 15s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility A, then) Glute Kickback → Scorpions → Superman Raises.

**Ref. Paces:** 5k 5:30–5:40/mi.

## Saturday — Workout A: Capacity Intervals

**Theme:** Aerobic capacity; 10k “on,” AE “off.”

WU: 18min easy + 4 strides (2× 3k, 2× Mile).

**4 Rounds of: (3min On @ 10k, 2min Off @ AE).**

CD: STR/Mobility D. 10 Reps or 20s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility C, then) Skipping → Lunge (FWD/Lateral) → Split SQ → V-Up.

**Ref. Paces:** 10k 5:50–6:00/mi. AE 6:50–7:20/mi.

## Sunday — Long Run

**Theme:** Aerobic conditioning.

10mi at even-pace. Pickup: last mile @ steady.

**Ref. Paces:** Long Run 7:30–8:45/mi. Steady 7:00–7:30/mi.
