# Distance Offseason — Block-4 — Ben Wurst

Block-4 shifts toward **“3k into mile”**: Wed = Workout A (e.g. 1k @ 10k–tempo or 5k/800s), Sat = Workout B anaerobic (4×(10×100m) then **3×(8×200m)** @ Goal-Mile). **Week 1 is meet week** (USATF All-Comers 3, 1600m). **Test in Week 4:** Kosmin (4×60s @ Goal-Mile).

**Ref. Paces (Block-4):** Goal-Mile 4:45–4:55/mi (or current target); 3k 5:05–5:20/mi; 5k 5:25–5:35/mi; 10k 5:45–5:55/mi; Tempo 5:55–6:05/mi; HM 6:05–6:15/mi; Easy > 8:45/mi; AE 6:45–7:15/mi; Long Run 7:25–8:40/mi; Steady 7:00–7:30/mi.

# Week 1 (4A) — Meet Week (1600m)

Theme: Aerobic power, 5k rhythm (5×800m), pre-race tune-up, then **1600m at USATF All-Comers 3** on Sunday.

## Monday — Medium A: Aerobic Power

**Theme:** 10k sustained efforts; 4×6min.

WU: 20–25min easy + 5 strides (2× 3k, 2× Mile, 1× 800).

**4 × 6min @ 10k.** Jog-Rest: 90s–2min p/rep.

CD: STR/Mobility C. 8 Reps or 15s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility A, then) Glute Kickback → Scorpions → Superman Raises.

**Ref. Paces:** 10k 5:45–5:55/mi.

## Tuesday — Weights (+ Optional AM-Jog or Easy Run)

**Theme:** Lower-body strength; optional aerobic maintenance.

AM-Jog or Easy Run: 20–35min (or 3–4mi) @ Easy

4×5 Squat | 3×8 RDL | 3×10 Step-Ups  
3×12 Russian Twist | 3×15 Standing Calf

**Ref. Paces:** Easy > 8:45/mi.

## Wednesday — Workout B: 5k Rhythm Reps

**Theme:** 5×800m @ 5k; optional faster last rep @ 3k.

WU: 22–24min easy + 4 strides (1× 5k, 2× 3k, 1× Mile).

**5 × 800m @ 5k** (optional faster last rep @ 3k). Jog-Rest: 400m p/rep.

CD: STR/Mobility D. 10 Reps or 20s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility C, then) Skipping → Lunge (FWD/Lateral) → Split SQ → V-Up.

**Ref. Paces:** 5k 5:25–5:35/mi. 3k 5:05–5:20/mi.

## Saturday — Medium B: Pre-Race Tune-Up

**Theme:** Speed drills and 1600m feel; 4×200 @ 1600m pace.

Speed Drills (Skips, Prime-Times for sure) → Progressive Accels | **4 × 200 @ 1600m pace** (full rest).

## Saturday — Test-Day: Kosmin Test

**Theme:** Kosmin test; 4×60s @ Goal-Mile. Use distance per rep to estimate aerobic power / mile readiness.

WU: 12–15min easy + 4 strides (2× 5k, 2× 3k).

**4 × 60s @ Goal-Mile**  
#1 → 3min Rest | #2 → 2min Rest | #3 → 60s Rest | #4

Make note of the distance traveled per rep. Can help to start the next rep from where the prior one finished.

Tactical notes: Spread out your effort, control the first two so you can hold strong in the back half. Empty the tank on Rep 4.

**Ref. Paces / conversion (example):** 2025 PR 4:48 → ≈1420m (~355m p/rep). Goal-Mile 4:30 → ~1500m (~375m p/rep).
