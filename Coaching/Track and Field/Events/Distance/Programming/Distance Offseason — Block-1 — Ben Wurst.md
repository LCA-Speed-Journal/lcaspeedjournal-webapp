# Distance Offseason — Block-1 — Ben Wurst

Block-1 eases the athlete out of soccer and rebuilds with 5k rhythm, tempo, and aerobic capacity. STR/Mobility A/B; optional Tue and light structure in early weeks. **Test in Week 4:** 5k time trial (e.g. 17:40 ±10s).

**Ref. Paces (Block-1):** 5k 5:30–5:40/mi; Tempo 6:00–6:10/mi; HM 6:10–6:20/mi; 10k 5:50–6:00/mi; M 6:25–6:40/mi; 3k 5:10–5:20/mi; Easy > 8:45/mi; AE 6:50–7:20/mi; Long Run 7:30–8:45/mi; Steady 7:00–7:30/mi.

---

# Week 1 (1A)

Theme: Transition from soccer; light structure. Two playoff games (Mon/Tue), then easy run, optional easy, 5k rhythm intervals, rebuilding intervals, and long run.

## Monday — Soccer Game: Playoff A

**Theme:** Game-day stress; counts as 2–3mi equivalent (average distance in a soccer game).

LCA vs HCPA. Counting as 2–3mi equivalent, as that’s the statistical average distance traveled in a soccer game. Could be higher-stress, could be lower, just going with an average representation.

## Tuesday — Soccer Game: Playoff B?

**Theme:** Optional second game-day; decide whether to start workouts tomorrow or take another day or two easy.

LCA vs MPA. Another game-day stress. Depending on how you’re feeling after today, can opt to start workouts tomorrow (left-column), or take it easy for another day or two (right-column).

## Wednesday — Easy Run

**Theme:** Flush post soccer-season; get the legs moving.

Easy Run (20–25min, no faster than AE [7:00/mi]). Aiming to just flush post soccer-season and get the legs moving for an extended interval. Not big.

**Ref. Paces:** (Easy) > 8:45/mi. AE 6:50–7:20/mi.

## Thursday — (Optional) Easy

**Theme:** Recovery or light add-on; optional strides.

Either OFF or Easy Run (as Wed) and possibly add some strides at the end for some speed (say, 2 @ 5k, 2 @ 3k).

**Ref. Paces:** (Easy) > 8:45/mi. AE 6:50–7:20/mi.

## Friday — Medium B: 5k Rhythm Intervals

**Theme:** Reintroduce 5k rhythm; low volume.

WU: 12–15min easy + 4 strides (2× 5k, 2× 3k).

**6 × 2min @ 5k pace.** Jog-Rest: 90s p/rep.

CD: STR/Mobility A. 8 Reps or 15s p/ea. Continuous. Plank (Side, Reg) → Tabletop March → Clamshell → Rev. Clamshell → Lat. Leg Raise → Knee Circles FWD/BKWD → Psoas-Raises.

**Ref. Paces:** 5k 5:30–5:40/mi. Jog (Easy) > 8:45/mi.

## Saturday — Workout B: Rebuilding Intervals

**Theme:** Aerobic capacity; 10k–tempo to AE.

WU: 12–15min easy + 4 strides (2× 5k, 2× 3k).

**5 Rounds of: (1min “On” @ 10k–Tempo, 5min “Off” @ AE).**

CD: STR/Mobility B. 10 Reps or 20s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility A, then) Walking Lunge + Twist → Good Morning → Cossack SQ.

**Ref. Paces:** Tempo to 10k 6:00–6:20/mi. AE 6:50–7:20/mi.

## Sunday — Long Run

**Theme:** Aerobic conditioning.

9mi at even-pace. No faster than Steady.

**Ref. Paces:** Long Run 7:30–8:45/mi (can be slower).

# Week 2 (1B)

Theme: Build structure; tempo, short hills, 5k rhythm reps, progression run, long run. Tue/Thu can add volume cautiously.

## Monday — Medium A: Tempo Run

**Theme:** Anaerobic conditioning at half-marathon effort.

WU: 20min Easy, straight into  
15min @ Half-Marathon.

CD: STR/Mobility A. 8 Reps or 15s p/ea. Continuous. Plank (Side, Reg) → Tabletop March → Clamshell → Rev. Clamshell → Lat. Leg Raise → Knee Circles FWD/BKWD → Psoas-Raises.

**Ref. Paces:** HM 6:10–6:20/mi. Easy > 8:45/mi.

## Tuesday — (Optional) Extra volume

**Theme:** Unless you already put some additional easy volume in Week 1, similar advice applies for Week 2: you can throw it in, but just be cautious about the additional volume.

## Wednesday — Workout A: Short Hills (Form)

**Theme:** Hill form; ~300–400m gradual incline at 5k effort.

WU: 12–15min easy + 4 strides (2× 5k, 2× 3k).

**5 × 300m uphill @ 5k.** Recovery: Jog down.

CD: STR/Mobility B. 10 Reps or 20s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility A, then) Walking Lunge + Twist → Good Morning → Cossack SQ.

**Ref. Paces:** 5k 5:30–5:40/mi.

## Thursday — Off or easy

**Theme:** Recovery between Wednesday and Friday quality.

## Friday — Medium B: 5k Rhythm Reps

**Theme:** 5k rhythm; moderate rep length.

WU: 15min easy + 4 strides (2× 5k, 2× 3k).

**5 × 3min @ 5k.** Jog-Rest: 90s p/rep.

CD: STR/Mobility A. 8 Reps or 15s p/ea. Continuous. Plank (Side, Reg) → Tabletop March → Clamshell → Rev. Clamshell → Lat. Leg Raise → Knee Circles FWD/BKWD → Psoas-Raises.

**Ref. Paces:** 5k 5:30–5:40/mi. Jog (Easy) > 8:45/mi.

## Saturday — Workout B: Progression Run

**Theme:** Progressive aerobic load; M → HM → 10k.

WU: 20min Easy, straight into  
15min @ Marathon → 10min @ HM → 5min @ 10k.  
Finish with 10min Easy.

**Ref. Paces:** M 6:30–6:40/mi. HM 6:10–6:20/mi. 10k 5:50–6:00/mi.

## Sunday — Long Run

**Theme:** Aerobic conditioning.

10mi at even-pace. No faster than Steady.

**Ref. Paces:** Long Run 7:30–8:45/mi (can be slower).

# Week 4 (1D) — Deload + 5k Time Trial

Theme: Deload and testing. Light tempo + strides, 5k rhythm intervals, easy progression, then 5k time trial on Saturday.

## Monday — Medium A: Tempo + Strides

**Theme:** Light anaerobic conditioning and leg turnover.

WU: 20min Easy, directly into  
8min @ HM. 4 × Strides (2 @ 5k, 1 @ 3k, 1 @ Mile).

CD: STR/Mobility A. 8 Reps or 15s p/ea. Continuous. Plank (Side, Reg) → Tabletop March → Clamshell → Rev. Clamshell → Lat. Leg Raise → Knee Circles FWD/BKWD → Psoas-Raises.

**Ref. Paces:** HM 6:10–6:20/mi.

## Tuesday — (Optional) AM-Jog or Easy Run

**Theme:** Optional easy volume before test week.

AM-Jog or Easy Run: 20–35min (or 3–4mi) @ Easy.

**Ref. Paces:** Easy > 8:45/mi.

## Wednesday — Medium B: 5k Rhythm Intervals

**Theme:** Light aerobic sharpening; moderate volume.

WU: 12min easy + 4 strides (2× 5k, 2× 3k).

**4 Rounds of: (2min “On” @ 5k, 2min “Off” @ Easy).**

CD: STR/Mobility A. 8 Reps or 15s p/ea. Continuous. Plank (Side, Reg) → Tabletop March → Clamshell → Rev. Clamshell → Lat. Leg Raise → Knee Circles FWD/BKWD → Psoas-Raises.

**Ref. Paces:** 5k 5:30–5:40/mi. Easy > 8:45/mi.

## Thursday — Weights (+ Optional AM-Jog or Easy Run)

**Theme:** Maintenance; total-body focus, reduced load before test.

AM-Jog or Easy Run: 20–35min (or 3–4mi) @ Easy  
————  
Weights (Total-Body Focus):  
3×5 Squat | 3× (8 Shoulder Press, 8 Lat-Pull)  
2×8 RDL | 3× (10 DB Bench, 10 DB Row)

**Ref. Paces:** Easy > 8:45/mi.

## Friday — Medium C: Easy Progression

**Theme:** Light progression; keep legs ready for Saturday test.

WU: 12min easy + 4 strides (2× 5k, 2× 3k).

8min @ Marathon → 6min @ HM.  
Cooldown with 10min Easy.

**Ref. Paces:** M 6:25–6:35/mi. HM 6:10–6:20/mi.

## Saturday — Test-Day: 5k Time Trial

**Theme:** 5k time trial; fitness check and pace reference.

WU: 12–15min easy + 4 strides (2× 5k, 2× 3k).

**Race-pace 5k effort.**

Tactical notes: Be cautious about coming out too aggressive. Conservative 1k, on-pace for next 3k, open it up for the last 1k. Start your final, finishing kick a little further back than you’re used to.

**Ref. Paces:** Target: 17:40 ±10s (5:35–5:45/mi).

## Sunday — Long Run

**Theme:** Easy aerobic wrap-up; deload volume.

9mi at even-pace. Pick up pace on last mile (to Steady).

**Ref. Paces:** Long Run 7:30–8:45/mi. Steady 7:00–7:30/mi.
