# Distance Offseason — Block-3 — Ben Wurst

Block-3 emphasizes 5k/10k and 3k work and introduces **first mile-paced work**: 4×(10×100m) at Goal-Mile and 3×300m @ 1500m. First **indoor meet** in Week 2 (1600m, USATF All-Comers 1). **Test in Week 4:** 5k time trial (e.g. 17:25 ±10s).

**Ref. Paces (Block-3):** Goal-Mile 4:45–4:55/mi; 3k 5:05–5:15/mi; 5k 5:25–5:35/mi; 10k 5:45–5:55/mi; HM 6:05–6:15/mi; AE 6:45–7:15/mi; Long Run 7:25–8:40/mi.

---

# Week 1 (3A)

Theme: 5k rhythm, capacity intervals, 3k rhythm reps. Weights Tue/Thu; Fri off or optional.

## Monday — Medium A: 5k Rhythm Intervals

**Theme:** 5k-paced sustained intervals.

WU: 15–18min easy + 4 strides (1× 5k, 2× 3k, 1× Mile).

**5 × 4min @ 5k pace.** Jog-Rest: 90s p/rep.

CD: STR/Mobility C. 8 Reps or 15s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility A, then) Glute Kickback → Scorpions → Superman Raises.

**Ref. Paces:** 5k 5:25–5:35/mi.

## Tuesday — Weights (+ Optional AM-Jog or Easy Run)

**Theme:** Lower-body strength; optional aerobic maintenance.

AM-Jog or Easy Run: 20–35min (or 3–4mi) @ Easy  
————  
Weights (Lower Focus):  
3×3 Seated Vertical Jump  
4×5 Squat | 3×8 RDL | 3×10 Step-Ups  
3×12 Russian Twist | 3×15 Standing Calf

**Ref. Paces:** Easy > 8:45/mi.

## Wednesday — Workout A: Capacity Intervals

**Theme:** Aerobic capacity; 10k “on,” AE “off.”

WU: 18min easy + 4 strides (1× 5k, 2× 3k, 1× Mile).

**5 Rounds of: (3min On @ 10k, 2:30 Off @ AE).**

CD: STR/Mobility D. 10 Reps or 20s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility C, then) Skipping → Lunge (FWD/Lateral) → Split SQ → V-Up.

**Ref. Paces:** 10k 5:45–5:55/mi. AE 6:45–7:15/mi.

## Thursday — Weights (+ Optional AM-Jog or Easy Run)

**Theme:** Total-body strength and power.

AM-Jog or Easy Run: 20–35min (or 3–4mi) @ Easy  
————  
Weights (Total-Body Focus):  
4×3 Hang Clean | 2×5 Trap-Bar DL  
3×8 (Shoulder Press, Lat-Pull) | 3×10 (BB Bench, DB Row)  
2 × (Triple Broad-Jump | 4× SL Hop | 10s Line-Hop)

**Ref. Paces:** Easy > 8:45/mi.

## Friday — Off or Optional Easy

**Theme:** Recovery before Saturday quality; optional easy only.

## Saturday — Workout B: 3k Rhythm Reps

**Theme:** 3k rhythm; round 1 @ 3k, round 2 ~1–2s faster.

WU: 18–20min easy + 4 strides (1× 5k, 2× 3k, 1× Mile).

**2 Rounds of: 3×300m.** Round 1 @ 3k, Round 2 1–2″ faster. Jog-Rest: 100m p/rep, 400m p/round.

CD: STR/Mobility D. 10 Reps or 20s p/ea. Continuous. (as STR/Mobility C, then) Skipping → Lunge (FWD/Lateral) → Split SQ → V-Up.

**Ref. Paces:** 3k 5:05–5:15/mi.

## Sunday — Long Run

**Theme:** Aerobic conditioning.

11mi at even-pace. Pickup: last mile @ steady.

**Ref. Paces:** Long Run 7:25–8:40/mi. Steady 7:00–7:30/mi.
