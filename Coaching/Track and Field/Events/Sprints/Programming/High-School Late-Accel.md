# High-School Late-Accel
## Warmups
- Drill-Copmplex: rudiment, skill, application. Super short turnaround between reps 
	- March → Single-Switch → Double-Switch → Triple-Switch + Bounce → 10yd Accel
- Small-Group Drilling: Groups of 3
	- Banded [[Yuri]] on Sled: Banded Support, Sled-Pushing, Load on Sled
## Short Accels
4-5 Steps to 5yd, 7 Steps to 10yd
	Attack in the first 4, then starting to rise
	Add a few extra (5 more steps, aiming for 20yd) and we’re starting to come up more
