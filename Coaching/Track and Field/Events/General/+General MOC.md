# General

General event technique and training resources

## Technique

- [[Ball and Socket Exercises]]: Exercises for training ball and socket joints (hips/shoulders) including weighted arm circles (swimming with DB) and banded external/internal rotation, associated with [[Austin Jochum]]'s movement approach.
- [[Norwegian Singles]]: Overview of Norwegian distance training system emphasizing sub-threshold training (2.5-3.5mmol lactate), consistency over intensity, building toward 2-3 sub-threshold sessions per week plus long run, with distance-based and time-based interval examples.
