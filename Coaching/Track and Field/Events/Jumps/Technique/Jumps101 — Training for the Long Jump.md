# Jumps101 — Training for the Long Jump
## Bounding
Bounding can develop power, timing, patience, and elasticity — all important for the long-jump.
Goal is to cover maximal distance per bound — big split, keep torso stacked over top.
## Drills and Exercises
- Dribble Runs
- Dribble Runs to Pop-Up
- Rocket Lunges — aiming for full extension when coming up
- Gallops
- Hurdle-Hops — making sure to land with a full-foot contact
- Jump to Bungee — place a low-height bungee partway into the pit
	- Let the athlete focus on keeping themselves in the air to clear it
