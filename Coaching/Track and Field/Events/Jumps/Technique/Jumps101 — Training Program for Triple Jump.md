## Cycle 3
### Day 1 — Jump Practice
- 4 x Runway-Runs from 100ft
- 6 x Hop-to-Step (2-6 Step)
- 6 x Step-to-Jump (2-6 Step)
- 4-6 x Short-Approach Triple-Jumps (4-10 Step)
#### Detail Drills:
Hop:
- Hop-Touch (standing, walking, run-in)
- Repeat-Hops — 3-4 Sets
Step:
- Walking Bounding (flat-footed drill)
- Hop-to-Bound from 50ft Board
Jump:
- Step to Jump Touch
- Step to Jump with Bungee
- Landing
- Long Jump to Bungee (on jump leg)
