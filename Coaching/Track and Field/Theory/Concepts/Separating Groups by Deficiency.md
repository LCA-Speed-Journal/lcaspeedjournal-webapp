### Breaking into Groups
Breaking into groups relative to High/Low Force Deficient, and High/Low Velocity Deficient

> [!NOTE]- Graphics for Group-Separation
> ![[Pasted image 20250915095853.png]]
> ![[Pasted image 20250915100105.png]]
#### 0 (Special Group): Very Poor Accel — <50% RF
60% vDec Push
Increase force capability towards early-accel
5-10yds, 8-10 Reps
- 1-Day Example: 
	- Accel on Field + Absolute STR / STR-Speed in Weightroom
- 2-Day Example: 
	- (as above, but 2x)
- 3-Day Example: 
	- (Days 1, 3 as above)
	- MaxV on Field + Speed-STR in Weightroom
#### 1: Strength-Speed — >50% RF, Tau 1.1+
50% vDec Shoulder
Power Emphasis, Increase force capability specifically in late-accel
15-20yds, 6-8 Reps
- 1-Day Example:
	- Accel on Field + Absolute PWR/STR-Speed in Weightroom
- 2-Day Example: 
	- (as above, but 2x)
- 3-Day Example:
	- (Days 1, 3 as above)
	- MaxV on Field + PWR/STR-Speed in Weightroom
#### 2: Power — >50% RF, Tau 1-1.1
50% vDec Waist
Power Emphasis, Increase force capability specifically in late-accel
15-20yds, 6-8 Reps
- 1-Day Example: 
	- Mixed-Stimulus on Field + Speed-STR in Weightroom
- 2-Day Example:
	- MaxV on Field + Speed-STR in Weightroom
	- Mixed-Stimulus on Field + Mixed-Stimulus in Weightroom
- 3-Day Example
	- MaxV on Field + Speed-STR in Weightroom
	- Accel on Field + Absolute STR/STR-Speed in Weightroom
	- MaxV on Field + Speed-STR in Weightroom
#### 3: Speed-Strength — >50% RF, Tau 0.9-1.1
25% vDec Waist
Speed-STR emphasis, increase force capability at high-speed
20-25yds, 4-6 Reps
- 1-Day Example: 
	- Mixed-Stimulus on Field + Speed-STR in Weightroom
- 2-Day Example:
	- MaxV on Field + Speed-STR in Weightroom
	- Mixed-Stimulus on Field + Mixed-Stimulus in Weightroom
- 3-Day Example
	- MaxV on Field + Speed-STR in Weightroom
	- Accel on Field + Absolute STR/STR-Speed in Weightroom
	- MaxV on Field + Speed-STR in Weightroom
#### 4: Velocity — >50% RF, Tau 0.9-1
5-10% vDec Waist (can also do overspeed or free-running)
Technical emphasis, High-V without interrupting mechanics
30yds, 3-5 Reps
- 1-Day Example
	- MaxV on Field + Reactive STR in Weight-Room
- 2-Day Example
	- MaxV on Field + Reactive STR in Weight-Room
	- Mixed-Stimulus on Field + Mixed-Stimulus in Weight-Room
- 3-Day Example
	- MaxV on Field + Reactive STR in Weight-Room
	- Accel-Day on Field + Absolute STR/STR-Speed in Weightroom
	- MaxV on Field + Reactive STR in Weight-Room
