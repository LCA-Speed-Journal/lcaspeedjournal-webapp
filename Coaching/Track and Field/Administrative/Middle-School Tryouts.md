# Metrics Explained:
## Speed (KEY-TEST): **30m Dash**
3 Primaries, 2 Secondaries. Covers both **RATE** and **MAG.** 
*Setup: Gates at 0, 5, 10, 20, 30.*
### **Primaries:** 
- Starting-Strength: 0-5m, 0-10m
- Acceleration: 0-30m
- Early MaxV: 20-30m
### **Secondaries:**
- Transitional MaxV: 10-20m
- Early-Acceleration: 0-20m
**Rank on Total-Time and Early Max-V.**
