| **ADVANCEMENT OF MIDDLE SCHOOL ATHLETES** There are two scenarios in which a 7th or 8th grader may advance to participate in athletics at the high school level.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | **Policy on Advancement of Middle School Athletes** <br>This policy outlines the conditions under which 7th and 8th grade students may advance to participate in high school athletics. <br>The goal is to ***ensure opportunities for student-athletes*** while maintaining fairness, team integrity, and alignment with MSHSL guidelines.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| ● Activities that are open or closed to 7th or 8th grade athletes are determined seasonally based on registration needs.<br><br>Participants will be allowed to advance if the following conditions are met: <br>○ Tryouts are arranged so that **all interested persons** may tryout <br>○ The advancement fills a **specific position necessary** to field a team or offer the activity <br>○ The advancement **does not typically displace 9-12th grade students** from co-curricular activities <br>○ P**arent/ Guardian approval** is obtained | **General Conditions for Advancement** <br>A middle school student-athlete (7th or 8th grade) may advance to the high school level if ***all*** of the following criteria are met: <br>1. **Open Tryouts:** Tryouts must be arranged so that *all interested athletes* may participate. <br>2. **Team Necessity:** Advancement is required *to fill a specific position* necessary to field a team or offer the activity. <br>3. **No Displacement:** Advancement *does not displace students in grades 9–12* from participating in the activity. <br>4. **Parental Approval:** Parent/guardian consent must be obtained. |
