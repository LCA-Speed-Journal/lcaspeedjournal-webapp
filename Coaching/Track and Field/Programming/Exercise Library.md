## Main Work

### Speed

#### Short Accelerations

- 10m Accel (Standing-Start) — 3-4x10m, full recovery (2-3min)
- 15m Accel (Standing-Start) — 3-4x15m, full recovery (2-3min)
- 15m Accel (Block-Start) — 4x15m, full recovery (2-3min)
- ½ Kneeling Start — 3x4-Steps
- ½ Kneel Chase Sprints — 2x ~20m (out the door), full recovery

#### Sled Work

- 10m Sled (Light-Medium Load) — 2-3x10m, 30-40% bodyweight
- 10m Sled (Medium Load) — 3-4x10m, 40-50% bodyweight, full recovery (2-3min)
- 10m Sled (Medium-Heavy Load) — 4x10m, 50-60% bodyweight
- Sled Accel-March — 3x10yd
- Heavy Sled-Marches — 3-5x10m, heavy load, full recovery (2-3min)

#### Resisted Sprints

- Resisted Sprint (Chain/Sled) — 3x15yd
- Resisted Sprint — 20yd at 30% vDec (or 25-35% BW)
- Contrast Sprints (Resisted → Unresisted) — 6x10yd + 6x20yd, 30s rest between, then longer between sets

#### Max Velocity Work

- 30m Dash — 2x30m, full recovery (3-4min)
- 30-40yd Sprints — 6x30yd, 3min rest
- Fly-10s — 4 rounds (10+10), 20yd total
- Fly-Runs — with 30m Accel-Zone
- Ladder Flys — Fly-10, Fly-20, Fly-30, all with 30m Accel
- Short Accels — 30-50m
- Buildups — 2x30yd (building from 70-80% into 90-95% effort)

#### Special Sprint Work

- 8-Step Accels — 2-3x (hurdlers)
- 8-Step + H1 (mini-hurdle) — 1x (hurdlers)
- 4-Step Drill — 2-3 reps
