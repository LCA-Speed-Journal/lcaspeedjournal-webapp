# Week 2 — Spring Break B
*5 Practices, all Constrained to the Gym — Aiming for 60min Sessions with Full-Team*
## Monday — Speed A (Short Accels)
### Warmup + Movement Prep
- Single-Leg Stand — 2min p/leg
- Movement Exploration
	- Duck-Walk to Roll (on Coach-Command) — to Opposite 3-Point Line (~18m)
	- Lateral Bear-Gallops
- ISO-Lunge Plyos (Yielding, Deep-Tier)
- (Hurdlers Break off after SL-Stand and do)
	- Seated Mobility A (Leg Lifts) — 2 x 5 p/leg
	- Seated Mobility B (Trail Leg Lifts) — 2 x 5 p/leg
	- Wall Mobility A (Lead-Leg) — 2 x 8 p/leg
	- Wall Mobility B (Trail Leg) — 2 x 8 p/leg
	- Walkovers (Lead Leg, Trail Leg) — 3 x 3 Hurdles
	- Skipovers (Lead Leg, Trail Leg) — 3 x 3 Hurdles
### CNS Priming (inspired from CSAC On-the-Field)
- (Whenever Hurdles finish with their warmup drills, integrate into the large group) 
- Light Pogos (N-S-E-W) — (5yd-Drill, 5yd-Walk Alternating) p/ea
- Deep-Tier (Yielding Lunge-Plyo) — 2 x 20 p/leg
- Bounce-Fire: Wallking — to 1/2 Court
- Bounce-Fire: Jogging (similar to Toe-Up Prime-Times) — to Opposite 3-Point Line
- A-Switch: Triples (Hands-on-Hips) — Full-Court
- Superset: Sled-March and Unresisted Accel — 2-3 Rounds
	- Heavy Sled (60-80% BW, or ~105lb) — 10m p/rep
	- 4-Step Accel: from 1/2-Kneel
### Main Work
- Med-Ball Relay (Working our resisted sprint idea, just in a more game-like manner) — Aim for 2 Reps
	- ~20lb Balls, two teams, racing between opposing free-throw lines to give enough room to decelerate after the handoff.
- 15m Accels (2pt Start) — Aim for 3 Reps
	- Coach Cue: “setup the torso where we want it on step one. From there, explode straight out and attack back.”
	- (Hurdlers — Split off for 2x 8-Step Accels from a crouch-start after doing 1x15m with Large Group)
- Active-Rest (between 15m Accel Reps) — Spikeball Trios, first to 5 Points
### Accessories
- Bilateral Calf-Bridge on Floor (Drive Midfoot-Pressure) — 2 x 30s
- Foam-Roller Hip-Hike (Stand on Plate, Pin Foam-Roller to Wall) — 2 x 20  Reps p/side
## Tuesday — Weights A (Intro and Statics)
### Warmup + Movement Prep
- SL Stand — 2mins p/leg
- Movement Game — Bear Crawl Capture-the-Flag
	- Partnered — Partner-A standing, Partner-B in a bear crawl with a towel tucked into their waist (or use flag-football belts). A tries to grab towel from B, B needs to try to dodge. No physical jostling.
- Hurdle Alternative (separate after SL Stand)
	- Wall Mobility A (Lead-Leg) — 2 x 8 p/leg
	- Wall Mobility B (Trail-Leg) — 2 x 8 p/leg
	- Walkovers (Lead Leg, Trail Leg) — 3 x 3 Hurdles
	- Skip-Overs (Lead Leg, Trail Leg) — 3 x 3 Hurdles
### CNS Priming
- ISO-Testing — Ankle-Dorsiflexion (1x 5s Test p/leg)
- Active-Recovery (when not ISO-Testing) — Two Stations
	- Plyomat Push-Up: Measuring Height
	- Infinity-Jog with Ball
### Main Work + Accessories (Combined into One-Block)
*Goal: Complete 2 Rounds of Full Circuit. 1 Round = 4mins per area, completing one set of all three exercises within.*
- Area A — Upper-Body (the most clustered, least likely to need coach supervision)
	- Deep Push-Up Hold (Upper-Push) — 40-50s
	- Dead Hang (Upper-Pull) — 40-50s
	- Handstand Hold (against wall) — 40-50s
- Area B — Sleds (taking up the most area, easy accessories for more independent work on those)
	- Sled-Marches, Heavy (Lower-Push) — 10-15m
	- ISO-Lunge with Floating Heel (Deep ROM) — 60s p/leg
	- Spring Ankle (Lower-Leg) — 40s p/leg
- Area C — Voltra (the most coach-involved, to make sure all movements are done to high quality)
	- Cable Kickback, Offset for Adductor (Lower-Pull) — 10 p/leg 
	- Psoas Raise (braced against wall) — 20-30s p/side
	- High Side-Plank (Core) — 40s p/side
