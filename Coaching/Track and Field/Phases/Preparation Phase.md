## For the Sprinters/Jumpers
### 24/28s Drill — 200m
*(Total Duration: )*
- Details:
- Setup:
	- Timing Gates — 1 Lane (0m, 50m, 100m, 150m, 200m)
	- Cones — p/5m from 165m-210m
		- Yellow — 165m, **170m**
			- Regular at 65m, Dome at 70m
		- Orange — 175m, **180m**
			- Regular at 75m, Dome at 80m
		- Green — 185m, **190m** 
			- Regular at 85m, Dome at 90m
		- Blue — 195m, **200m**
			- Regular at 95m, Dome at 200m
		- Purple — 205m, **210m**
			- Regular at 105m, Dome at 110m
- Execution: Split into **2 Groups** — Complete Entire Test, then Switch-Groups
	- Finish Flags: 
		- Hold Yellow Flag when Test in-Progress
		- Hold Green Flag when Next Athlete Cleared to Run (after gate-reset)
		- Hold Red Flag if Problem with Entry (gate errors, etc.)
- Tracking/Display:
	- Tracking: Paper Data-Sheet for manual backup, writing down athlete-distances at 24s/28s
		- Sixth Gate in Spectator Mode, on Master Table for easy Data-Entry
	- Leaderboard: Total Distance, Key Splits
		- Total Distance (m)
		- 0-100m (s)
		- 50-100m (mph)
		- 100-150m (mph)
		- 0-150m (s)

## For the Distance Athletes
### Kosmin Test (800m Prediction)
*(Total Duration: 5mins, Post-Warmup)*
- Details: 2 x 60 secs for max-distance (Rest-Period: 3min)
- Setup:
	- Colored Cones — Key Distances denoting a threshold of performance
		- Developmental (2:30 or slower) — ***Blue*, 300m**
		- JV / Varsity Transition (2:20-2:30) — ***Green*, 330m**
		- Solid-Performer / Sectional-Viability (2:15-2:20) — ***Yellow*, 350m**
		- Championship Competency (2:08-2:15) — ***Orange*, 375m**
		- **MCAA** Podium-Threat (2:04-2:08) — ***Red,* 390m**
		- **State**-Qualifying (2:00-2:02) — ***Purple*, 410m**
		- **State**-Finalist / Elite (1:59 or faster) — ***Purple*, 425m**
	- Regular Cones (9x) — Round, ~10m landmarks
		- Developmental — 250m, 260m, 280m
		- JV/V Transition — 320m
		- Section-Viability — 340m
		- Champ Competency — 360m
		- MCAA Podium — 380m
		- State-Qual — 400m
		- State-Finals — 420m
	- Pylons (19x) — separating into 5m segments to help for measurement purposes
		- Developmental — 265, 270, 275 || 285, 290, 295
		- JV/V Transition — 305, 310, 315 || 325
		- Section-Viability — 335 || 345
		- Champ Competency — 355 || 365, 370
		- MCAA Podium — 385 || 395
		- State-Qual — 405 || 415
- Execution: Split into **2-4 Groups** — Complete Entire Test, then Switch-Groups
	- Group A (Slower-Group) — Runs 1st, then Measures for B
	- Group B (Faster-Group) — Measures for A, then Runs 2nd
- Tracking/Prediction:
	- Tracking: Index Cards p/athlete (name on back for easy ID)
		- Master Table entering marks into the spreadsheet — athletes come up and report
	- Leaderboard: Total Distance, Best Rep

# Schedule — Week by Week
## Week 1 — First Day(s) at the Track
*5 Practices, Friday on the Track — 60min Practice-Blocks (75min on Monday)*
*Splitting into A and B-Session Practice Groups after Monday-Orientation*
First week with athletes back in school is always weird, since we usually jump heavily in numbers. Need to continue introducing concepts, but some have already been here for 2wks over break.
### Structure
- M — Speed (Full-Team Practice)
	- Warmup:
		- ISOs: SL Stand or ISO-Lunge (90s-2min per leg)
		- Mobility: Lunge + Reach (or Lunge + Twist)
		- Deep-Tier: Lunge-Pulses
	- CNS-Priming:
		- Extensive: Pogos, Bounce-Fire Series
		- Constraint Work: Hands on Hips A-Series (2x15m)
		- Resisted Drills: Sled-Marches (Light-Medium load, 2-3x15m)
		- 4-Step Acceleration Drill
	- Main Work
		- Contrast Sprints (Medium Sled → 15m Accels)
		- *Hurdlers:* 8-Step Accelerations
- T — Weights-Day (Full-Team Practice)
	- **A-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Ankle Dorsiflexion
		- Weights-Work: 1x20 Circuit
	- **B-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Ankle Dorsiflexion, Hamstring Curl
		- Weights-Work: 1x20 Circuit
- W — X-Factor
	- **A-Session:**
		-  Testing: CMJ, Triple Broad Jump
		- *Hurdlers:* 8-Step Accelerations
		- Jumps-Help:
			- Horizontals: Gallops , Cone-Drill walk-through
			- Verticals: Curved-Bounds, Pop-Ups
	- **B-Session:**
		- Testing: CMJ, Triple Broad Jump
		- Jumps-Help: 
			- Horizontals: Gallops, Cone-Drill for Hop-Phase
			- Verticals: Curved-Bounds, Altitude-Lands
- Th — Weights-Day (Active-Recovery)
	- **A-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Hamstring Curl
		- Weights-Work: 1x20 Circuit
	- **B-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Adduction + Hip-Extension
		- Weights-Work: 1x20 Circuit
- F — Lactic (Full-Team Practice, First Day at the Track)
	- Group Warmup: Basic Speed-Drills
	- **Technique Breakout (Before Lactic Work):**
		- Sprinters: Extended Warmup (more Drills) + Block-Starts (10m Reps)
		- *Hurdlers:* 8-Step Acceleration, Acceleration + Hurdle-1
		- Horizontal Jumpers: Short Approaches, Cone-Drill for Hop-Phase
		- Vertical Jumpers: Curved-Runs, Pop-Ups
	- **Lactic Work:** 24/28s Drill
## Week 2 — Short-Week (Good-Friday)
*4 Practices, Monday on the Track — 60min A/B-Session Practices, No Practice on Friday*
### Structure
- M — Speed (Full-Team Practice at the Track)
	- Group Warmup: Basic Speed-Drills
		- A-Series with PVC Overhead
		- Skips for Length
	- **Technique Groups:**
		- Sprinters: Fly-10’s with a 30m Build-In
			- Before getting into those, hit some Long Prime-Times and the Dribble-Series
		- *Hurdlers:* 8-Step Acceleration, Acceleration + Hurdle-1
		- Horizontal Jumpers: Short Approaches, Cone-Drill for Hop-Phase, Hop-to-Pit
		- Vertical Jumpers: Curved-Runs, Pop-Ups, Short-Approaches
- T — Weights-Day (Active-Recovery)
	- **A-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Adduction
		- Weights-Work: Last Day of 1x20 Circuit
	- **B-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Dorsiflexion + Hamstring-Curl
		- Weights-Work: 1x15 Circuit
- W — X-Factor
	- **A-Session:**
		- Testing: CMJ, Standing Triple Jump
		- Jumps-Help: 
			- Horizontals: Pop-Ups, Cone-Drill for Hop-Phase, Basic Hop-Bounds
			- Verticals: Curve-Runs, Pop-Ups
	- **B-Session:**
		- Testing: CMJ, Standing Triple Jump
		- Jumps-Help:
			- Horizontals: Pop-Ups, Cone-Drill for Hop-Phase, Continuous Hop-Bounds
			- Verticals: Curve-Runs, Pop-Ups
- Th — Speed
	- **A-Session:**
		- Warmup + Movement Prep:
			- Dynamic Hip Warmup (as [[Dynamic Hip Sequence]])
		- CNS Priming
			- Extensive: Pogos, Bounce-Fire Series (Walking, Jogging)
			- Sled-Marches
			- Sled-Bounds
			- Contrast: Medium Sled-Sprints → 4-Step Accel Drill
		- Main Work:
			- Curved-Sprint Contrast (Curve-Sprint, 15m Linear Acceleration)
			- *Hurdlers:* 8-Step Acceleration, Acceleration + Hurdle-1
	- **B-Session:**
		- Warmup + Movement Prep:
			- Dynamic Hip Warmup (as [[Dynamic Hip Sequence]])
		- CNS Priming
			- Extensive: Pogos, Bounce-Fire Series (Walking, Jogging)
			- Sled-Marches
			- Sled-Bounds
			- Contrast: Medium Sled-Sprints → 4-Step Accel Drill
		- Main Work:
			- Curved-Sprint Contrast (Curve-Sprint, 15m Linear Acceleration)
- F — NO PRACTICE
## Week 3 — Meet 1 (Gryphon 1) on Wednesday
*4 Practices + 1 Meet, M/F on the Track — 60min A/B-Session Practices, Meet on Wednesday*
Week of Season Opener. Gauge overall team-fatigue coming in, as well as in response to first comp — make adjustments to Tues/Thurs as necessary.
### Structure
- M — Speed (Full Team at the Track)
	- Group Warmup: Basic Speed-Drills
		- Toe-Pops
		- A-Series with Hands Overhead
		- Prime Times for Length
	- **Technique Groups:**
		- Sprinters: Block Starts (30m Acceleration), Relay Handoffs
			- Before getting into those, hit the Dribble-Series and some Constraint-Sprints
		- *Hurdlers:* 8-Step Acceleration, Acceleration + Hurdle-1, Acceleration + Hurdles 1-2
		- Horizontal Jumpers: Short Approaches, Hop-to-Pit, Hop-Step-to-Pit
		- Vertical Jumpers: Pop-Ups, Short-Approaches, Full-Approaches
- T — Weights-Day (Active-Recovery)
	- **A-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Hip Extension
		- Weights-Work: 1x15 Circuit
	- **B-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Adduction + Hip-Extension
		- Weights-Work: 1x15 Circuit
- W — Meet-Day
	- Treat as High-Effort Practice, just getting the feel of comp-season opening up.
- Th — Weights-Day (Active-Recovery)
	- **A-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Dorsiflexion + Hamstring-Curl
		- Weights-Work: 1x15 Circuit
	- **B-Session:**
		- ISO-Test: Dorsiflexion + Hamstring-Curl
		- Weights-Work: 1x10 Circuit
- F — Lactic (Full Team at the Track)
	- Group Warmup: Basic Speed-Drills
		- A-Series with Weighted PVC or Med-Ball Overhead
		- Skips for Height and for Length
		- Prime Times for Speed and for Length
	- **Technique Groups:**
		- Sprinters: Contrast Sprints (20m Resisted → 30m Unresisted)
			- Before getting into these, hit some No-Arm Sprints
		- *Hurdlers:* 8-Step Acceleration, Acceleration + Hurdle-1, Acceleration + Hurdles 1-2
		- Horizontal Jumpers: Full Approaches, Hop-Step-to-Pit
		- Vertical Jumpers: Pop-Ups, Short-Approaches, Full-Approaches
