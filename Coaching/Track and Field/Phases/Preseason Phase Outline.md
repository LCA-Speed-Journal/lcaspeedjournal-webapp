# Week 1 — Spring Break A
*5 Days, all Constrained to the Gym — Aiming for 60min Sessions with Full-Team*
Making sure everyone moves well, looking for struggling patterns, rebuilding ISO strength lost in offseason. Running the Track & Field Combine on W/F (F being Speed-Trials), so expect bigger attendance on those days.

## Monday — Speed A (Short Accels)

### Warmup + Movement Prep (10-15mins)
- **Mobility Work**
	- [[Dynamic Hip Sequence]] (as per [[David Grey]])
	- Foot/Ankle mobility — [[Foot and Achilles Rehab]]
	- T-Spine mobility
- **ISO-Holds**
	- Long Yielding — SL-Stand, ISO-Lunge (analgesic effect)
	- Medium, Force-Capacity — fRoller Bridge
	- Short, OVC — for potentiation
- **Dynamic Warmup**
	- Thermodynamic: light skipping, shuffling, accels at 70%
	- Dynamic stretches: high-knee pulls, straight-leg kicks, lunge and reach
	- Backwards series: walking, skipping, running

### CNS Priming (6-10mins)
- **Easier Drills**
	- A-Series (March, Skips, "Booms," Switches)
	- Pogos — bilateral and unilateral variations
	- Toe-Pops — SL, Bilateral + Weight, Alternating
- **Progressing Drills**
	- A-Skips for height, for length, for speed
	- Hip-Hike Actions — Crawling, Lateral High-Knees, Wall-Hikes
	- Rapid high-knees
- **Accel-Specific Priming**
	- Horizontal Series drills
	- Transfer Series
	- Light resisted marches or bounds

### Main Work (20-30mins)
- **3x 10m Sled (Medium–Load)**
	- Focus on horizontal force application
	- Full recovery between reps
- **3x 15m Accel (Standing-Start)**
	- Focus on progressive body rise
	- Full recovery between reps
	- *Hurdlers* go first — 1x 15m, then leave for 2x 8-Step Accels

### Accessories and Structure (6-10mins)
- **Foot/Lower-Leg**
	- Spring Ankle variations
	- Calf raises or foot strengthening
- **Core**
	- High Side-Plank
	- Core stability work
- **Hip Stability**
	- Banded work as needed
	- Hip flexor/glute activation

## Tuesday — Weights A (Intro and Statics)

### Warmup + Movement Prep (10-15mins)
- **Mobility Work**
	- [[Dynamic Hip Sequence]]
	- Ankle mobility work
	- Upper body mobility — [[Upper Body Basics]]
- **Banded Activation**

	- Standing Series: Monster-Walks, Diagonal Walks, H. Flexor Raises

	- Light calisthenics

### CNS Priming (6-10mins)
- **Light Movement Prep Only**
	- This is a low-intensity day (per [[High-Low Model]])
	- Focus on movement quality, not CNS activation
	- Light dynamic movements to prepare for isometric work

### Main Work (20-30mins)
- **ISO-Test — Ankle Dorsiflexion (RSIST)**
- **Upper-Push — Deep Push-Up Hold**
- **Upper-Pull — Dead Hang**
- **Lower-Push — Sled-Marches (Heavy)**
- **Lower-Pull — Cable Kickback (for Adductor)**
- **Core — High Side-Plank**
- **Lower-Leg — Spring Ankle**

### Accessories and Structure (6-10mins)
- **Additional Structure Work**
	- Individualized based on assessments
	- Address any struggling patterns identified
	- Additional isometric holds as needed
