”**The program is open to all 7th through 12th grade boys and girls who want to participate.** 
	Practices are held in the Auxiliary Gym and on the track here on campus. 
	**This team practices as one unit and then competes by gender in groups according to their ability.** 
Students are asked to provide their own shoes, competition uniforms will be supplied. 
The track team competes as a member of the Skyline Conference. In addition the team participates in 6 to 8 meets throughout the season.”
