MSA offers the following varsity-level sports for students. Registration fees apply. 
Participation in these sports is **open to students in grades 7 through 12 unless otherwise noted.** 
Girls are allowed to participate on boys’ teams if an all-girls team is not offered for a specific sport. 
	* — Indicates there will be a separate middle school team (grades 6-8) for this sport that will compete against other middle school teams in the area.
