# Middle School Athletics
**TCA TIGERS Middle School SPORTS**
We hope to offer the following sports here at Twin Cities Academy for middle school:
##### **FALL**: 
- MS Soccer (We partner with Great River- all practices are over there.)
- HS Cross Country (grades 7-12)
- HS Football (7th & 8th grade eligible for the HS program) 
- HS Volleyball (7th & 8th grade eligible for the HS program, if physically capable) 
##### **WINTER**:
- MS Basketball starts in November.
- HS Basketball (7th & 8th grade eligible for the HS program, if physically capable) 
- Boys’ Hockey (JV only, grades 8-12, begins November )
##### **WINTER/SPRING**:
- Co-ed Volleyball (grades 6-8) 
##### **SPRING**:
- HS Softball (grades 7-12)
- **HS Track (grades 7-12)**
