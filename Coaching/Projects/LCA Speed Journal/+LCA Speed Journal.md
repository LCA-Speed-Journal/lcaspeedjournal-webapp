# Basic Overview
a Python-driven app designed for the track and field team to efficiently enter, store, and visualize key metrics across all athletes. 
## Content:
The project has three primary functions:
1. **Data Intake**: Form-based data entry pushing to Google Sheets
2. **Leaderboard Visualization**: Live-updating leaderboards that parse data from Google Sheets, ranking and organizing athlete performance metrics
3. **Detailed Visualization**: Complex visualizations from all historical data including:
	- Progression charts
	- All-time and by-mesocycle leaders
	- Athlete dashboards
