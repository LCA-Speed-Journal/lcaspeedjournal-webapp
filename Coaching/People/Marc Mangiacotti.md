# Big Ideas

## Complements to Speed Training Menu
Harvard coach providing comprehensive training menu for speed development.

### Accel Menu
- Short Hill/Stadium Runs (12-15 Reps)
- 3-4 x 10, 20, 30m (various starts)
- 3x4 x 15, 25, 35m (various starts)
- 12x30m or 10x40m Resisted-Runs (Sled-Work)
- Longer Stick-Drills (up to 30-40m)
- Combo Work (contrast stuff, resisted/unresisted)

### MaxV Menu
- Fly 10's, 20's, 30's
- In's and Out's (10-20m Segments)
- Advanced Wickets
- Light Assisted Runs (ExerGenie)
- Light Downhill Running (small % grade)

### Speed Development
- 9x50m or 8x60m
- **Sprint-Float-Sprint**:
  - 4-5 x 90m — 30/30/30
  - 4-5 x 90m — 50/20/20
  - 3x120m — 40/40/40
  - 2-3 x150m — 50/50/50
- Runs + Elastic-Work:
  - 150, 120, 60 + run, skip, or bound
  - 120, 90, 70 + run, skip, or bound
  - 80, 70, 60 + run, skip, or bound

### Speed Endurance
#### Short Speed-Endurance
- 4 x 4x60m with 2-3min / 6-8min recoveries
- 4 x 4x60m with 1min / 4-6min recoveries
- 6x80m with 8min recovery

#### Long-Speed Endurance
- 150, 120, 90, 60 with 10-12min recovery
- 150, 120, 3-4 x 60m with 10min between longs and 1-2min between shorts
- 3-4 x 150m with 10min recovery
- Hybrid: 2 x 2x150m with 1min / 10min recoveries
- Race-Modeling: up to 180m

### Special Endurance Protocols
- **Special Endurance 1 Menu**: 180, 150, 120m; 2-3 x Fly-100's with 20m Build; etc.
- **Special Endurance 2 Menu**: 2 x 150m with 12min recovery; 3 x 150m with full-recovery; etc.

### Multi-Jumps
- **Acceleration**: Standing Long-Jump, Standing Triple-Jump, Concentric Box-Jumps, Hurdle-Hops, Vertical-Jump Testing
- **Max-V**: Repeat-Bounding, Eccentric Box-Jumps, Eccentric Box-Jumps into Dynamic Hurdle-Hops

### Multi-Throws
- Underhand Forward
- Overhead Backwards
- Med-Ball Slams
- Hop-Hop Overhead Back / Underhand FWD

### Weight-Room
- Cleans / Snatches
- Squats / DL (Trap-Bar)
- Jump-Squat

### Warmup Differences
- **MaxV vs Acceleration**: Different warmup protocols for each
- **Acceleration**: Joint Mobility, Dynamic Mobility, Accel Drills, Accel-Specific Drills
- **Max-Velocity**: Joint Mobility, Glute-Activation, Movement Exercises, Specific Strength and Mobility, Technical Builds

### Superblocks
- **Acceleration**: Hurdle-Hops, Block-Starts
- **MaxV**: Hop-Hop-OHB with light-implement, Fly-10 (30m build)

# Web-Summary

Marc Mangiacotti is a Harvard coach known for providing comprehensive training menus for speed development, with detailed protocols for acceleration, max velocity, speed development, speed endurance, special endurance, multi-jumps, multi-throws, and weight room work. His approach includes sophisticated sprint-float-sprint progressions (various distance combinations like 30/30/30, 50/20/20, 40/40/40, 50/50/50) and runs combined with elastic work. Mangiacotti's system distinguishes between short and long speed-endurance protocols, with specific recovery intervals and volume recommendations. He provides detailed warmup differences between max-velocity and acceleration sessions, recognizing that each requires different preparation protocols. His work includes superblock applications for both acceleration (hurdle-hops, block-starts) and max-velocity (hop-hop overhead back with light implement, fly-10s with 30m build). Through his comprehensive training menus and systematic approach to speed development, Mangiacotti has established a detailed resource for coaches seeking structured speed training protocols across multiple training qualities.
