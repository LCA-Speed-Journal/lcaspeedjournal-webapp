# Big Ideas

## General to Specific Speed
Athletes Acceleration general to specific speed approach.

### Core Concepts
- **Escape or Chase** - General Sport Concept: Athletes need to make decision quickly
- **Good Techniques and Posture**
- **The Magic of Sport** - Live-Action and Predicting Movement
  - These inform which corrective-drills you do - patterns athletes use when *reacting* to things
- **If you want Athletes to Improve, need to Force Them to Compete**

### Live-Drilling: Programming
- Great as Warmups - can use as Movement Screen, gets athletes more involved/engaged, narrows focus
- Good as part of Whole-Part Approach, seeing what natural tendencies of athletes are before putting corrective-drills on top
