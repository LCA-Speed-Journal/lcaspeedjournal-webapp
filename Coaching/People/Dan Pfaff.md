# Web-Summary

Dan Pfaff is a renowned jumps coach whose warmup structure has been widely adopted and modified by other coaches, including Keinan Briggs. His comprehensive jumps warmup includes A-skip, B-skip, backwards skipping, backwards walking, lateral walking, crossovers, high-knees, dribble-series, long prime-times, bent-knee prime-times, single-leg recoveries, leg-swings, hurdle-recoveries, and strides. Through his systematic approach to warmup design and his influence on the coaching community, Pfaff has established foundational warmup protocols that prepare athletes for jumps training while addressing mobility, coordination, and technical preparation.
