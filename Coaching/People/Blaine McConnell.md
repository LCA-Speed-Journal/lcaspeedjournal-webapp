# Web-Summary

Blaine McConnell is a coach associated with UNIT 2.0 and UNIT + Hoss training programs, collaborating with Will Ratelle on the UNIT + Hoss program. These programs represent systematic approaches to athletic development, with particular emphasis on comprehensive training systems that address multiple athletic qualities. Through his work on UNIT programs, McConnell has contributed to establishing structured training methodologies for athletic performance development.
