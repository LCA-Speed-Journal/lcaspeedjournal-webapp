# Web-Summary

Shawn Sherman is the creator of the Square1 System, a comprehensive movement and performance system organizing chapters covering introduction, perspective, human movement system, functional anatomy, neural response muscle testing, steps of Square1 System, mimicking the ground, isometrics, and Square1 Level 2. Through his systematic approach to movement and performance, Sherman has established a comprehensive framework for understanding and developing human movement capabilities, with particular emphasis on neural response testing, isometric training, and ground-based movement principles. His work through the Square1 System and Signal 6 Course has contributed to advancing understanding of movement assessment and performance development.
