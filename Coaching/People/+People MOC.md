# People

- [[Alec Holler]]: Hurdle coach known for discount hurdling approach (practicing at lower heights and distances) and low-impact daily drills focusing on wall drills, walkovers, and skip-overs for technical development.
- [[Austin Jochum]]: Coach emphasizing play-based training and movement games, host of Jochum Strength Podcast and creator of Building Better Movers program.
- [[Blaine McConnell]]: Coach associated with UNIT 2.0 and UNIT + Hoss training programs.
- [[Cal Dietz]]: Creator of Triphasic Training system and RPR (Reflexive Performance Reset), involved with Revolutions in Speed educational events.
- [[Chris Korfist]]: Speed coach and co-founder of TFC (Track Football Consortium) with Tony Holler, also involved with Revolutions in Speed events and YouTube lecture series.
- [[Danny Foley]]: Coach focused on fascial training concepts and methodologies.
- [[Dario Saisan (SaisanTraining)]]: Coach known for delayed knee extension concepts and the Saisan Training System, associated with Adarian Barr's sprint programming.
- [[David Grey]]: Physical therapist and coach with expertise in dynamic hip sequences, lower/upper body basics, foot and Achilles rehabilitation, and core training basics.
- [[HansenSprint]]: Coaching resource focused on hamstring rehabilitation using the 10 x 10m protocol.
- [[Jake Tuura]]: Researcher and coach with expertise in patellar tendinopathy research, hypertrophy clusters, jumpers knee protocol, vertical jump protocol, and high-frequency training.
- [[Joe Rubio]]: Distance running coach known for Principles of Elite 1500m Training methodology.
- [[Lachen Reid]]: Coach and program creator associated with Racehorse 2 training program.
- [[Les Spellman]]: Founder of Universal Speed Rating system, a widely endorsed speed measurement and rating tool.
- [[Past Athletes]]: Notes on past athletes including Sophia Anderson, Colin Anderson, and Tom Kanowitz, documenting their training influences and methodologies.
- [[Steve Magness]]: Author of The Science of Running, providing research-based insights into running performance and training.
- [[Tim Riley]]: Host of Coach Em Up podcast and creator of Cheat Code training program, associated with Austin Jochum and Jake Tuura.
- [[Tony Holler]]: Founder of Feed the Cats training philosophy, co-founder of TFC (Track Football Consortium) with Chris Korfist, and large proponent of RPR (Reflexive Performance Reset).
