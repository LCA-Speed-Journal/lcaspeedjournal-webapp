# Web-Summary

Tony Veney is a USATF Master Coach specializing in training the intermediate hurdles (300m hurdles), with a comprehensive approach that emphasizes acceleration, speed development, and speed endurance as the keys to success. His event modeling breaks the race into three distinct sections: hurdles 1-3 (adrenaline), hurdles 4-6 (race development), and hurdles 7-8 (capacity). Veney's technical elements include specific arm action mechanics for intermediate hurdles, steering practice for maintaining rhythm and spacing, and strength endurance training methods specific to the 300m hurdles. Through his systematic approach to intermediate hurdle training and his understanding of the unique demands of the event, Veney has established himself as a leading authority on developing intermediate hurdlers.
