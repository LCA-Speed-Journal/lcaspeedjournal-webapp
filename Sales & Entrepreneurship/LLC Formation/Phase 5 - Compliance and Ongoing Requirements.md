# Phase 5: Compliance and Ongoing Requirements

## Minnesota Annual Renewal
- [ ] Mark calendar: Annual renewal due each year (**free online**)
- [ ] Set reminder 30 days before due date
- [ ] File online at mblsportal.sos.state.mn.us

## Registered Agent Service
- [ ] **Year 1**: Free with Inc Authority
- [ ] **Year 2+**: Budget for registered agent service ($125-$250/year)
- [ ] Options: Inc Authority, Northwest Registered Agent, or switch to another provider

## Tax Obligations
- [ ] Quarterly estimated taxes (if applicable)
- [ ] Annual tax return (Schedule C on personal return for single-member LLC)
- [ ] Sales tax returns (if registered for sales tax)
- [ ] Keep detailed records of all income and expenses

## Business Records Maintenance
- [ ] Keep Articles of Organization
- [ ] Maintain Operating Agreement
- [ ] Keep all tax documents and returns
- [ ] Maintain bank statements and financial records
- [ ] Keep contracts and service agreements
- [ ] Document all business decisions (meeting minutes, if applicable)

## Licenses and Permits (Verify as Needed)
- [ ] Check city/county business license requirements
- [ ] Verify no special permits needed for online coaching
- [ ] Confirm contractor work doesn't require additional licensing
- [ ] *Note: Online coaching is not regulated; 1:1 coaching compliance met if needed*
