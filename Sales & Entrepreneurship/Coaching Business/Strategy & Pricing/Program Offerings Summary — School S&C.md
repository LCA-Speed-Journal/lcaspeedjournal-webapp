# Program Offerings Summary: School-Sponsored S&C Programs

**See [[Pricing Model - School-Sponsored S&C Programs]] for complete details and sales framework.**

---

## Program Offerings Overview

### 12-Week Seasonal Programs (School Year)
- **Fall Session**: September-November
- **Winter Session**: December-February
- **Spring Session**: March-May
- **Frequency**: 2-3 sessions per week (rotating schedule, sessions offered daily)
- **Session Duration**: 45 minutes
- **Schedule**: 1 before-school session + 2 after-school sessions daily

**Pricing:**
- **In-Season Athletes**: $40-$60 add-on to athletic fees
- **Out-of-Season Athletes**: **$180-$220 per athlete**

**Expected Enrollment:**
- **Fall**: 41-45 in-season athletes
- **Winter**: 38 in-season athletes
- **Spring**: 36-42 in-season athletes
- **Out-of-Season**: 12-15 athletes per season (break-even benchmark)

---

### 8-Week Off-Season Programs
- **Summer Strength & Conditioning**: June-August (8 weeks, 4-5×/week)
- **Winter Strength & Conditioning**: November-December or January-February (8 weeks, 4-5×/week)
- **Frequency**: 4-5 sessions per week (more intensive, structured blocks)
- **Session Duration**: 60 minutes

**Pricing:**
- **In-Season Athletes**: $40-$60 add-on to athletic fees
- **Out-of-Season Athletes**: **TBD** (priced separately, more intensive)

---

### Specialized Intensive Camps (6-Week Programs)

#### Winter Speed Development (Add-On Program)
- **Duration**: 6 weeks
- **Frequency**: 2 additional sessions per week on top of normal S&C (5-6 sessions/week total)
- **Focus**: Speed, agility, and power development
- **Format**: Add-on (athletes must be enrolled in regular winter S&C)
- **Pricing**: **$150-$200 per athlete**
- **Target Enrollment**: 10-15 athletes

#### Summer All-In Program A (Standalone)
- **Duration**: 6 weeks
- **Frequency**: 4-5 sessions per week
- **Total Sessions**: 24-30 sessions
- **Focus**: Comprehensive athletic development (strength, speed, conditioning)
- **Format**: Standalone intensive program
- **Pricing**: **$300-$400 per athlete**
- **Target Enrollment**: 12-24 athletes

#### Summer All-In Program B (Standalone)
- **Duration**: 6 weeks
- **Frequency**: 4-5 sessions per week
- **Total Sessions**: 24-30 sessions
- **Focus**: Comprehensive athletic development (strength, speed, conditioning)
- **Format**: Standalone intensive program
- **Pricing**: **$300-$400 per athlete**
- **Target Enrollment**: 12-24 athletes
- **Multi-Program Discount**: 10% off second program if doing both

---

## Revenue Distribution Model

**Compensation Structure:**
- **Coaches (Head + Assistant)**: 70% of total program revenue
- **School Facility/Overhead**: 20% of total program revenue
- **Program Costs (Equipment, Admin, etc.)**: 10% of total program revenue
