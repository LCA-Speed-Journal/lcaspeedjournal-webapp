# Personal Training Business Outline
## High School Athletes - Minnesota Market

## Business Context

**Operation**: Side hustle (afternoons/evenings), operates around 7am-3pm day job
**Target Market**: High school athletes in Minnesota (offseason development)
**Positioning**: Certified High School Strength & Conditioning Coach (NHSSCC), specializing in programming
**Starting Point**: No established client list (1 interested athlete), extensive programming experience, group/team coaching background

**Key Constraints:**
- No facility (must work around box gym restrictions)
- Limited 1:1 experience (group/team coaching background)
- Side hustle operation (limited availability)
- Not certified personal trainer (CPT) - use NHSSCC credential

**Strategic Focus:**
1. **Tier 1: Remote Coaching** (Primary focus - plays to strengths)
2. **Tier 2: Hybrid Coaching** (Secondary focus - balanced approach)
3. **Tier 3: 1:1 In-Person** (De-emphasize initially due to constraints)

## Revenue Model: Sequence of Offers

### 1. Attraction Mechanism ("Get Cash")
- **Discovery Session**: Free 30-minute consultation/assessment ($100 value)
- **Trial Offer**: 2-week remote program trial - $99 (applies to first package if they commit)
- **Referral Bonus**: Refer 2 athletes, get 1 month free

### 2. Upsells ("Get More Cash")
- **Add-On Sessions**: Additional technique sessions at discounted rate
- **Nutrition Coaching**: +$150/month (meal planning, macro tracking)
- **Recruiting Support**: +$200 (highlight film editing, college outreach)
- **Sport-Specific Skill Work**: +$100-200/month (depending on frequency)

### 3. Downsells ("Get the Most Cash")
- **Payment Plans**: Monthly payment options (no discount, but removes barrier)
- **Feature Downsell**: If hybrid too expensive, offer remote-only with option to add sessions later
- **Frequency Adjustment**: If 5×/week too much, offer 3×/week at lower price

### 4. Continuity ("Get the Most Cash" - Sustained)
- **Annual Commitment**: 10-15% discount for booking all 3 seasonal packages upfront
- **Rollover Discounts**: Complete one package, get 5% off next package
- **Multi-Package Commitment**: 12.5% discount for booking 2+ seasonal packages upfront

## Value Proposition & Selling Points

### Competitive Advantages to Emphasize
1. **Certified High School S&C Coach** (NHSSCC) - specific credential for target market
2. **Track Record**: 2 NCAA athletes, state qualifiers, over 20 sectional finalists
3. **Programming Expertise**: 10 years in strength & conditioning field
4. **Perfect Timing**: Afternoon/evening availability aligns with athlete schedules
5. **Flexible Model**: Remote/hybrid options fit athlete's schedule and location
6. **Offseason Specialization**: Focused on development during prime training windows

### Value Equation Application
**Increase Dream Outcome:** Position as pathway to college athletics, improved performance, injury prevention
**Increase Perceived Likelihood:** Highlight credentials, success stories (2 NCAA athletes, state qualifiers), track record
**Decrease Time Delay:** Emphasize offseason = prime development window, structured blocks with clear timelines
**Decrease Effort & Sacrifice:** Payment plans, sibling discounts (10% second child, 15% third+), upfront package discounts

### Key Selling Messages
- **For ETS-aware athletes**: "Like ETS's data-driven approach? We offer similar performance tracking with more personalized attention and flexibility."
- **For budget-conscious families**: "Instead of facility visits 2-4×/week, get customized programming you can do on your schedule, plus regular check-ins."
- **For sport coaches**: "We specialize in offseason development with seasonal packages designed around your competitive calendar."

## Pricing Strategy

### Initial Pricing (First 3-5 Clients - "Founding Members")
- **Start 10-15% below full rates** to build client base and testimonials
- Position as "early adopter" benefit
- Set expectation: Rates will increase to market pricing after building client base
- Use early clients to generate testimonials, case studies, and referrals

### Pricing Modifiers
- **Sibling Discount**: 10% off second child, 15% off third+ children
- **Early Bird Pricing**: 5% additional discount for booking 30+ days before program start
- **Geographic Adjustments**: Metro (upper end), Suburban (mid-range), Rural (lower end)
- **Credential Premium**: Position in upper 25% of market ranges (justified by NHSSCC, track record)

### Market Benchmarks
- **ETS Virtual**: $400-$1,000/month (online coaching) - competitive positioning opportunity
- **MN Local Remote**: $120-$200/month (competitors)
- **MN Hybrid**: $275-$300/month (competitors)
- **Your Positioning**: Mid-to-premium range ($250-$600/month for hybrid standard)

## Guarantees & Risk Reduction

### Results Guarantee
If athlete follows program and doesn't see improvement in key metrics (strength, speed, performance), offer 2 additional sessions free

### Satisfaction Guarantee
If not satisfied after first 2 weeks, refund remaining package (minus trial session if used)

## Implementation Priorities

### Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-3)
1. **Focus on Tier 1 (Remote Coaching)** - Primary offering
2. **Offer "Founding Member" pricing** (10-15% discount) for first 3-5 clients
3. **Collect testimonials and case studies** from early clients
4. **Build proof** of results before raising rates

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-6)
1. **Introduce Tier 2 (Hybrid Coaching)** as secondary offering
2. **Raise rates to market pricing** after 5+ clients and proof of results
3. **Offer seasonal packages** (Summer Development first)
4. **Add upsells** (nutrition coaching, recruiting support)

### Phase 3: Expansion (Months 7-12)
1. **Launch all seasonal packages** (Summer, Winter, Preseason)
2. **Build client base** to 10-15 athletes
3. **Consider Tier 3 (1:1)** if facility options become available
4. **Scale systems** for client management and communication
