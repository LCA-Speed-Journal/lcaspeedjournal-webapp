# Lower — Gabe Imm (8-Week Program)

**Session type:** Lower Body (Squat & Hinge)  
**Use on:** Lower day of the week (e.g. Tuesday).  
**Total time:** 50–60 minutes.

**How to run:** Move through each section in order. Warm-up & Movement Prep (10–15 min), then Athletic Movement Patterns if you have time (5–10 min). Main Strength Work: straight sets with 2–3 min rest; Weeks 3–6 include 1×AMRAP @ ~80%. Secondaries: single-leg and posterior-chain complements. Accessories/Structure: lower-leg, core, hip. If form breaks, stop and note it.
