# Phase 2: LLC Formation with Inc Authority

## LLC Formation Process

### Visit Inc Authority Website and Start LLC Formation
- [ ] Select "Free LLC Formation" option
- [ ] Enter business name (ensure it matches your research)
- [ ] Select Minnesota as formation state
- [ ] Choose "Member-Managed" (since you're operating solo)

### Complete Inc Authority Forms
- [ ] Provide business address (can use home address or registered agent address)
- [ ] Designate registered agent (Inc Authority provides free for 1st year)
- [ ] List yourself as the sole member/manager
- [ ] Select business purpose (fitness coaching, strength & conditioning services, online programming)

### Review and Pay
- [ ] Review all information for accuracy
- [ ] Pay Minnesota state filing fee: **$155** (online filing)
- [ ] Decline optional add-ons initially (Operating Agreement, record book, website) — you can add later if needed
- [ ] Submit formation documents

### Wait for Processing
- [ ] Inc Authority typically takes 5-7 business days
- [ ] You'll receive confirmation email when filed
- [ ] Minnesota processes online filings in 2-5 business days
- [ ] **Total timeline: ~7-12 business days**
