# $100M Money Models

## Web Summary

"$100M Money Models" is about turning attention and leads into scalable, predictable, compounding revenue. Hormozi shows how to build multi-layered, cash-flow-generating infrastructures in business.

The core idea is creating a **Money Model** with three stages:
1. **"Get Cash"** via front-end offers - attract cash quickly
2. **"Get More Cash"** via upsells & downsells - increase customer value
3. **"Get the Most Cash"** through continuity - sustain maximum cash via subscriptions/memberships

Key principles:
- Every offer must recover acquisition costs quickly (ideally within 30 days)
- Stacking predictable offer layers eliminates the cash constraints that throttle growth
- Roll out one stage at a time and perfect it rather than implementing all at once
- Focus on fewer offers done extremely well instead of many average ones
- Closing gaps via affiliate partnerships helps without bloating operations
