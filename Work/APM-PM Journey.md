# APM-PM Journey
Currently 14 key skills needed for an APM to master before they are considered for promotion to PM
## APM “Levels”
Two, or Three, depends on who you ask and how they want it to be structured.
Paired with compensation, allows for a clear path of advancement.
- Better solution than simply time-based consideration for promotion between the levels of APM competence.
	- (4.9yrs is…a lot)
### Level 1
Foundational Knowledge, Fundamentals — what are the “must-know” skills for an APM?
### Level 2
Project-Specific Needs, “Electives” — does this APM need to know more about (insert skill)
### Level 3
…dunno about this one. 
Likely more specific skills, bridging the gap to higher-level management.
