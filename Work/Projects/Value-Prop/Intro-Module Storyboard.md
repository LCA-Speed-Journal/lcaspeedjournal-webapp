**Slide Title:** Core Values

**Content:**
*Voiceover:* "Our core values define our company and our culture."

*On-screen text:* Each value appears with its description:

- **Embrace the Edge** — Approach challenges and change with confidence. Operate at the edge of what's possible and pursue continuous growth and improvement.
